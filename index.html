Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the builder, hosting, custom domain mappings, and the self-serve CMS.

How does the AI website generation work?
NexSite integrates with Grok AI to analyze details you enter through our WhatsApp-like bot chat (e.g. name, services, styling preference). The AI designs professional copywriting, reviews lists, and customizes FAQs tailored to your industry niche, injecting it directly into our responsive layout templates.
Can I modify the generated content afterward?
Yes! During the publish/checkout phase, you will define an Admin Email and Password. You can use these credentials to log in to your personal NexSite CMS Dashboard. From there, you can edit header texts, stories, service lists, phone numbers, map links, and template themes instantly with a live visual editor.
How do I receive leads from my published website?
Each template features a functional contact form. Every time a visitor fills in their details (name, email, message), the form invokes our backend route and stores the entry safely in your Supabase database. You can view all captured leads inside your CMS dashboard in real-time.
Do you support custom domain mappings?
Custom domain support is available on our Premium Plan. Once you publish your site, you can link your own domain (e.g. www.mybusiness.com) by pointing its DNS CNAME records to our hosting servers, which our support team will configure for you.
Is hosting included in the pricing?
Yes, high-speed, secure static hosting is fully covered in our monthly subscription fees (₹59/mo for Starter, ₹99/mo for Premium). We compile your page structures statically, ensuring lightning-fast LCP metrics and top search engine visibility.
